About Me

Hello! My name is Abida.

I'm an early-career IT professional with hands-on experience in IT support, user provisioning, account administration, and troubleshooting, alongside a growing focus on Identity & Access Management (IAM) and cloud security.

I build enterprise-style lab environments using Microsoft 365, Microsoft Entra ID, Active Directory, Windows Server, and Spiceworks to simulate real IT support operations. My projects cover everything from onboarding users and managing permissions to resolving support tickets and implementing identity security controls.

My goal is to build practical experience that mirrors the responsibilities of Help Desk, IT Support, and Systems Administration roles while continuing to expand into identity and cloud security.
