About Me

Identity & Access Management • Cybersecurity • Cloud Labs

Hello, my name Abida!

I am an early-career IT professional focused on Identity & Access Management (IAM) and cybersecurity. I build hands-on lab environments using Microsoft Entra ID, Microsoft 365, and Active Directory to develop practical experience in securing modern identity systems.

My work centers around implementing role-based access control (RBAC), Conditional Access policies, and authentication security aligned with Zero Trust principles. Through these labs, I develop skills in troubleshooting authentication flows, analyzing sign-in activity, and protecting user access.

I am working toward a career in cybersecurity, with a focus on identity security and security operations, and continuously improving through real-world simulations.
