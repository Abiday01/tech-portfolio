About Me

Hello! My name is Abida.

I'm an early-career IT professional with hands-on experience in IT support, user provisioning, account administration, and troubleshooting, alongside a growing focus on Identity & Access Management (IAM) and cloud security. I earned the Google IT Support Professional Certificate to build a strong foundation in technical support, operating systems, networking, system administration, and security fundamentals.

I build enterprise-style IT lab environments using Microsoft 365, Microsoft Entra ID, Active Directory, Windows Server, and Spiceworks to simulate real-world IT support and system administration workflows.

My goal is to continue growing into Help Desk, IT Support, and Systems Administration roles where I can apply these skills in a professional IT environment while continuing to expand my knowledge of identity, cloud, and cybersecurity technologies.
